Attractions and Amenities

Park Attractions

Roller Coaster

Need a thrill? Hop onto our famous roller coaster for a fast paced, exhilirating experience!

Arcade

Enjoy neon-lights and classic games? Go wild in our arcade!

Ferris Wheel

Prefer slower-paced rides? See the world from the highest Ferris Wheel in the world. 170m high!

Petting Zoo

Not interested in rides? Play with our adorable animals in our petting zoo. Sheep, goats, and cute little piggies. Be sure to read their bios!

Cowboy Shootout

Westerns? Of course, gunslinger! Shoot some bottles and win a prize at our western shooting range!

Water Slide

Feeling a bit hot? Shoot down our water slides and cool off during these hot summer days!

Amenities

Looking for a bite? Try one of our spectacularly built themed restaurants, or perhaps stop on by a food truck or fast food joint, here's a list of what to expect (click to view more information):

  • Our famous Roller Coaster themed restaurant
  • 6 different types of bistros
  • 12 different Fast-foods
  • We're proud to partner with local aspiring chefs, be on the look out for food trucks!

With plenty of restrooms and cash facilities throughout the park, you'll never have to wait in line to get back to that awesome ride.

Be on the look out for sign-posts throughout the park to discover relevant amenities.
